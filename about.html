Hi there, I guess you want to know me better? Okay, so I'll keep it short as I'm not too good in writing a personal page about me and I won't bother you with a whole biography. That's something for a writer.

My name is Glenn Van Bogaert. I am a 19 year old lad who finished school this year, June 2011. Currently I live in St. Niklaas with my parents. I love all things design and have an eye for expensive designer furniture. Rock and punk music brings me the inspiration as well as a lot of other music. I'm not bounded to any style. I can enjoy biking through cities, countrysides etc.

I started designing back in 2007 and a lot changed back since then. Without any doubt I made some progress in the design community and people recognize that too. I'm blessed with an invite to a closed community of some well known designers like Veerle Pieters, Tim Van Damme, Rogie King, Shaun Inman, Trent Walton, etc. This way, I can even grow more every day. The best of all is that I can keep contact with them and ask questions or get feedback through mail. They never disappointed me.

Of course I want to do more with the skills I gained in those 4 years. Creativity, enthusiasm and precision are my assets in the challenge for finding a job at a design agency. But most of all, everyday I'm inspired and motivated to make the web a better place.
